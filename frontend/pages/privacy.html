Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

1. Data We Collect 2. How We Use It 3. Contact-Form Data 4. Cookies 5. Your Rights 6. Contact Us

D Fashion Mart ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect through this website, why, and how you can control it.

1. Data We Collect

We only collect information you choose to give us:

  • Your name, phone number, and optionally your email address, when you submit our contact form.
  • The message content you type into the contact form.
  • Basic, anonymous website usage data (such as which pages are visited) if analytics are enabled.

2. How We Use It

We use the information you submit only to respond to your enquiry — for example, to confirm stock, arrange a fitting, or answer a question about the store. We do not sell or rent your information to third parties.

3. Contact-Form Data

When you submit the contact form, your details are sent directly to the shop's email/messaging inbox so a team member can reply. We keep this information only as long as needed to handle your enquiry, then delete it.

4. Cookies

This site uses only the minimum technical storage needed for it to function (for example, remembering that you've closed the mobile menu). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

5. Your Rights

You can ask us at any time what information we hold about you, request a correction, or ask us to delete it. Just contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to ganuj4769@gmail.com or by visiting us in store at NH 27, near Corporation Bank, Junab Ganj, Sarai Sahjadi, U.P. 226401.
